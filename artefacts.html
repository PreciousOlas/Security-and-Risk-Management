Pampered Pets Group Report

This artefact presents my individual contribution to the final group report for the Pampered Pets case study, developed as part of the Security and Risk Management module. This report was used alongside my team members in order to formulate one single report and aimed to demonstrates my ability to implement risk frameworks to assess the digital transformation of the Pampered Pets business.

View Group Report (PDF)

Reflective Commentary:

The feedback recognised my understanding of Pampered Pets’ business model and appropriate use of ISO 31000 and NIST CSF frameworks. However, it highlighted key areas necessary for improvement, including ensuring to make comparisons of frameworks such as OCTAVE or FAIR,the implementation of a Gantt chart, and requirement for a deeper critical evaluation. I have made reflections on this by considering how I can deepen my analytical writing and ensure consistent referencing in future projects.

Reflective Commentary

The feedback recognised my understanding of Pampered Pets’ business model and effective use of ISO 31000 and NIST CSF frameworks. However, it also identified areas for improvement, including comparing frameworks such as OCTAVE or FAIR, adding a Gantt chart, and providing a deeper critical evaluation. I have reflected on these points by focusing on improving analytical writing, expanding comparisons, and ensuring consistent use of Harvard referencing in future projects.
